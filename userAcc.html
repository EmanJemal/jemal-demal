Choose Your Team

Web Developers Team

Alright, let’s dive into web development! It’s all about how you bring your ideas to life. We’ll hook you up with templates, but we need your flair for designing the user interface (you know, what users actually see—images, typography, all that jazz!) and the user experience (how smooth and fun it is for users—think animations and the number of pages!). Your mission is to create something that resonates with our specific audience and purpose. Let’s make it awesome together!

Innovation and Research Team

Now, let’s chat about innovation and research! It’s all about finding the right solutions for the right problems at just the right moment. Remember, innovation isn’t the same as invention. It’s more about building on what’s already out there and adapting it to fit our needs. And right now, Ethiopia could really use this! If you’re on the research and innovation team, your mission is to find out when others have implemented certain solutions and when they might be needed here. Your goal? To roll out effective solutions that have worked elsewhere but with your own creative twist! Let’s get thinking outside the box!

Marketing and Promotion Team

Hey there! So, let’s talk about marketing and promotion. It’s all about figuring out the coolest way to sell a product from a specific provider while building a trustworthy vibe for the company. You’ve got to adapt to your surroundings and the current trends. Think of it like this: you can use different types of designs to make your point! For example, graphic design is perfect for eye-catching posters, fun flyers, awesome websites, and so much more! Also, it’s super important to dive into the Habesha community. Understanding what they need to trust a brand is key! Plus, connecting with experienced mentors who know the ins and outs of Habesha culture will be a game-changer. You’ll get to share experiences with them, and trust me, there will be dedicated time for that!
